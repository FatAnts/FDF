ControlFocusHighlight

Format: ControlFocusHighlight FrameName,

Example: ControlFocusHighlight "IconicButtonFocusHighlightTemplate",

Purpose: Define the HIGHLIGHT-Frame beeing displayed while the parentFrame has focus. A Button should have ControlStyle "HIGHLIGHTONFOCUS" to use this.

Used in: BUTTON, GLUEBUTTON, TEXTBUTTON, GLUETEXTBUTTON
