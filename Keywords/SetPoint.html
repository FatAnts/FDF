SetPoint

Format: SetPoint FramePoint-Own, FrameName , FramePoint-FrameName, xOffset , yOffset,

Example: SetPoint LEFT, "UpperButtonBarAlliesButton", RIGHT, 0, 0,

Example: SetPoint TOPLEFT, "InfoPanelIconAllyWoodIcon", BOTTOMLEFT, 0.0, 0.0,

Purpose: Pos the frame with its own FramePoint to FramePoint-FrameName with xOffset and yOffset (+x is right +y is up), UseActiveContext can alter the result. When the Frame is created the Frame basicly calls BlzFrameSetPoint(self, FramePoint-Own, BlzGetFrameByName(FrameName, 0), FramePoint-FrameName, x, y). When having UseActiveContext, in the frame with SetPoint it replaces 0 inside BlzGetFrameByName with the CreationContext of the current create Frame.

Used in: EveryWhere
